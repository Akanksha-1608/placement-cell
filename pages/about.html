Our Placement Cell

The Placement Cell is the official body that bridges students with the professional world. We organize placement drives, training workshops, resume sessions, and mock interviews throughout the year.

Our dedicated team maintains strong ties with 120+ companies across IT, Finance, Core Engineering and Management sectors — ensuring every student gets a genuine shot at their dream career.
