❓ Quick FAQ

How do I register for placements?

Click "Student Login", register with your college email, and fill your academic profile.

What is the eligibility criteria?

Minimum 60% aggregate and no active backlogs. Criteria may vary per company.

How do I stay updated on drives?

Log in regularly and check "Jobs & Announcements". Notifications are also sent by email.

Who to contact for portal issues?

Email placement@college.edu with your registration number and issue description.
